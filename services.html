HOW I WORK

Direct, practical, and evidence-driven.

1. Define the end state

Establish what success looks like before debating individual products, commands, or certifications.

2. Establish the current state

Identify existing skills, systems, constraints, risks, and missing information without pretending the environment is cleaner than it is.

3. Execute the next move

Turn the work into a sequence of achievable actions rather than a vague long-term ambition.

4. Validate and document

The result must work, be explainable, and leave behind evidence that supports future operations or career growth.

BOUNDARIES

What this service is and is not.

Engagements are educational or advisory and independently delivered. I do not provide managed IT operations, emergency support, legal advice, compliance certification, unauthorized security testing, or guarantees that a system cannot be compromised. Employer systems, branding, confidential information, and internal documentation are not used.
